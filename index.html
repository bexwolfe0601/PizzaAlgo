Bex Wolfe

I am a 25 year old, divorced, single mother. My life flipped upside down recently in the best way. This course is the start of a new life for myself and my son. I used to live in Oceanside, California so I love anything related to the ocean including but not limited to surfing, swimming, sunsets, bonfires, and great mexican food. Moving up here to Idaho, I have discovered new things to enjoy such as the season of fall, a supportive community atmosphere, and sledding down snowy slopes with my son. I have always been one who lives by a set of processes, I suppose that is what drew me to coding. It is appealing to me the sense of following rules, and being able to acheive comprehension enough to add creativity into my work. This is a new adventure for myself, and I am looking forward to where the road ahead leads.
